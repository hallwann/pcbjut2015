Introduction of RoboCup competition

RoboCup is an international scientific initiative with the goal to advance the state of the art of intelligent robots. When established in 1997, the original mission was to field a team of robots capable of winning against the human soccer World Cup champions by 2050. While that mission remains, RoboCup has since expanded into many countries around the world.

RoboCup promotes the transition of novel, world-class research results and technological advances from the laboratory to the real world by organizing regional and international competitions. The RoboCup competitions provide an excellent channel for the dissemination and validation of innovative concepts and approaches for autonomous robots under very challenging and adverse conditions, in order to encourage advances in important new directions.

MSL soccer robot ,atypicallmechatronic system, is an ideal platform for teachingresearch and engineering training, which, combines modeling technology, machinery, electronics,sensors, computer hardware and software, artificial intelligence and other advantaged technology.
